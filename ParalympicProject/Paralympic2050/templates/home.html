About the Paralympic Game

MILANO CORTINA 2026

Milano Cortina 2026 is a paralympic winter game which will open on the 6th of March 2026 in the Arena di Verona. This game will take place in Italy with more than 600 athletes competiting in 79 medal events across 6 sports. The 6 sports include Para alpine skiing, Para biathlon, Para cross-country skiing, Para ice hockey, Para snowboard, and wheelchair curling.

For more information, please visit:
