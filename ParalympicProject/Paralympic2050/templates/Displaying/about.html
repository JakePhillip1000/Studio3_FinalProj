The Paralympic Games

Paralymic games are international sport events for athletes with physical disabilities and first established since 1960. Similarly, both Paralympic and Olympic games held every four years. This bring together the world's best athletes to compete in differernt sports. The event are held in same year and same city as the Olympic games. Most of the paralympic sport, the athletes are grouped into classes based on their balance, coorfination and skills of each sport
